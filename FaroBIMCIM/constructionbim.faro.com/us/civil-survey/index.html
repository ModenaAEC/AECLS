From Site Surveys to CAD

FARO’s® high-precision Laser Scanners provide surveyors with complimentary technology to traditional surveying equipment. In addition FARO’s software plug’ins for AutoCAD® Software and Autodesk® Revit® allow processing scan data into various final CAD deliverables.

  • Capture Existing Conditions
  • Monitor Job Progress
  • Quality Assurance

Measurements in Any Surrounding

FARO Lasers Scanners provide surveyors a safe alternative to quickly scan busy work sites under challenging and rough conditions. With an extended temperature range and IP rating of class 54, FARO’s latest devices are protected against environmental influences, such as rain, dust, extreme heat or cold. In addition, FARO’s handheld scanners allow AEC customer to measure heavy accessible areas.

Provide Progress Reporting

Utilizing FARO laser scan technology allows surveyors to measure, manage and report job quantities. The extracted 3D data provides an accurate foundation to calculate volumetric, area and linear-based quantities.

Design Grade Verification

FARO 3D solutions serves as a basis at each phase of the project to supply design grade checks and reports. The point cloud can provide detailed as-built information in order to conduct a comparison to the design model for grade verification and required tolerances.

FocusS

Ranging Error* ± 1mm at 10m, Scanning range from 0.6m to 70m, 150m and 350m, Supports real time, on-site registration in SCENE

The FocusS combines hallmark features of the previous FARO® Focus3D product line with significant technological innovations such as Ingress Protection Rating (IP54), increased scanning accuracy, an internal accessory bay and a built-in compensation routine.

  • Transmit scan data wirelessly to FARO SCENE to perform real time, on-site registration
  • Rescan distant targets to perform accurate target detection or capture smaller areas in greater detail**
  • Capture outdoors with the certified industry standard Ingress Protection (IP) Rating, IP54
  • Scan with improved accuracy and distance via dual-axis compensator and angular measurement
  • Conduct on-site compensation routines for high data quality assurance
  • Operate the scanner in temperatures as low as -4°F (-20°C) and up to 131°F (55°C)

*Ranging error is defined as a systematic measurement error at around 10m and 25m, one sigma.

Freestyle3D X

Accuracy of ± 1mm, Scanning range from .5m to 3m, Intuitive plug and play system

The FARO Freestyle3D X is a top-quality, durable, industrial-grade and high-precision handheld scanner for AEC professionals. It quickly and reliably documents rooms, structures and objects in 3D and creates high-definition point clouds.

  • Scan around corners where visibility and lighting conditions is limited
  • Capture many surfaces in a wide range of environments with or without artificial targets
  • Calibrate on-site, ensuring consistent and high quality data
  • Combine results from Focus Laser Scanners and the Freestyle3D X, even in gray scale

Road-Scanner C

Compact Mobile Mapping System, Complete project planning, Includes powerful ergonomic software suite

With the Road-Scanner C FARO offers it’s customers the possibility to utilize a trusted mobile mapping system from Siteco. It is the only available mobile mapping system where the laser scanners can also be detached and use as stand-alone scanners.

  • Compact design, portable, self-calibrating and can be used with any vehicle
  • Post-processing software for imagery and point cloud management
  • Provides AutoCAD, ArcGIS, Microstation plug-ins
  • Road Assets and Pavement Management Systems

FARO SCENE

Align scans using real time on-site registration / Measure, 3D visualize, export scan data / Utilize many plug-in apps

FARO’s SCENE software is specifically designed for FocusS Series Laser Scanners and Freestyle Scanners. SCENE processes and manages scan data efficiently and easily by using real time, on-site registration, automatic object recognition, scan registration and positioning.

  • Real time, on-site registration for FocusS scan data accelerates reality capture
  • Laser High Dynamic Range (Laser-HDRTM) support – create impressive HDR quality images in a substantially short amount of time
  • Powerful 3D mesh engine allows for automatic modeling of free form shapes
  • Intuitive user interface offers guidance for complex tasks
  • Powerful Virtual Reality View enables management of 3D scan data in immersive detail

FARO SCENE WebShare Cloud

Hosting Service provided by FARO / Secure collaboration / 3D data sharing in the cloud

With SCENE WebShare Cloud, FARO offers a cloud-based service to provide users with simple access to 3D documentation. Due to the highest data security standards project data can be surely shared amongs clients and partners worldwide.

  • Easy-to-use 3D viewing of as-built data over the internet
  • Seamless integration with the SCENE software
  • SSL-encrypted communication
  • Support for tablet computers and smartphones

FARO As-Built for Revit®

Creating efficiencies in data extraction for BIM. Quick, intuitive & automated workflows. Wide range of evaluation tools

The FARO As-Built for Revit® software is specially designed for architects, engineers and general constructors that need to process 3D laser scan data quick and precise directly inside Autodesk® Revit® to create 3D and intelligent BIM models.

  • Benefit by tools for easy cropping and orientation within in the point cloud.
  • Create 3D models directly in the point cloud using a wide range of 3D construction aids
  • Quickly and precisely create walls, pipes and structural elements like beams and columns
  • Compare your model with reality using the surface analysis tool
  • Process scan data directly in the Revit® family editor

FARO As-Built for AutoCAD® Software

Data evaluation with highest precision, Versatile reality to CAD solutions, Automatic workflows under user control

As-Built for AutoCAD® offers all the functionality that AEC professionals need to evaluate 3D laser scanner data directly in AutoCAD®. The software can also handle data from various other measurement sensors, such drones, cameras or total stations.

  • Easily manage large 3D scan projects to keep an overview and control on your project
  • Fast and easily extract 2D plans and complete 3D models
  • Model piping systems and steel construction by intuitive and automated workflows
  • Analyze and visualize deviations of CAD models and point clouds for accuracy verification
  • Use photogrammetry in AutoCAD®: Evaluate data captured by laser scanners, drones or cameras
  • Combine spatial data with CAD objects and structured room schedules
  • Connect a total station on-site to AutoCAD® to draw floor plans, sections and elevations

FARO BuildIT Construction

Validate to Design / Perform Tolerance Evaluation / Position and Monitor

BuildIT Construction is the first fully integrated building lifecycle Quality Assurance/Control (QA/QC) management tool for construction professionals to continuously evaluate projects with real time comparisons against CAD designs using 3D scan data.

  • Verify accuracy of scan data to design models
  • Detect incorrect placement or missing features such as walls, columns, beams, pipes, etc…
  • Perform important measurements accurately and quickly
  • Inspect construction for adherence to building standards
  • Verify shifts and movements within structural performance over time with 4D analysis
  • Perform real time monitoring of adjacent structures throughout the project lifecycle
  • Import scan data from various laser scanner manufacturers and CAD platforms from file types

Data Connect & Share: FARO solutions integrate the data of different measurement methods used in surveying. They work with standard file formats and facilitate data exchange. FARO Cloud solutions enable the secure, worldwide exchange of 3D data and models and collaborative work in real time.

Quality Control: FARO workflows compare CAD and 3D measurement data and identify deviations. The deviations are visualized in an easy-to-digest image or output as computable values. Deviations in construction can be corrected on-site or taken into account in further planning.

Design Layout: FARO hardware and software solutions together enable the display of modelled or drawn objects into the real world.
The position of components and assembly points are determined, prefabrication is aided and CAD geometry is marked out in reality.

As-Built Model & Design
As-Built Model & Design: FARO software solutions enable the efficient evaluation of 3D measurement data from different sensor types. These help with the conversion to 3D models for further downstream use. The solutions work directly in industry-leading design software tools with simple, intuitive workflows.

Onsite capture:

Site features are captured quickly, accurately and easily using FARO hardware. In a BIM process, understanding what you have to start with or what has been built is essential.
